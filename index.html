How it works

Custom Snapchat Geofilters Made Simple

Describe

Submit and order detailing what'd you like on your snapchat filter. Tell us what you want and describe your event. You can be as detailed or minimilistic with it as you want. Created just for you!

Design

Your snapchat filter will be submitted to one of our creative geniuses. Our in-house graphic artists are one of a kind and have an eye for designing perfect Geofilter just for you.

Broadcast

On the day of your event, your GeoFilter will be hosted right on SnapChat! Anyone within the custom GeoFence of your event will be able to view and use your custom filter!

Snap!

You're all set! Take pictures with your awesome GeoFilter and make sure you send a few to us at SnapEasy on Snapchat! Tag us on instagram and twitter as well!
